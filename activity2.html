Codingal

is a fast-growing, mission-driven online coding platform dedicated to teaching children aged 5 to 18 computer science and technology skills. Founded in 2020 by IIT alumni, it operates globally from its headquarters in Bengaluru, Karnataka, empowering over 500,000 students with live 1-on-1 classes and interactive courses in Scratch, Python, AI, and game development. By using a standards-aligned, STEM.org accredited curriculum, Codingal makes programming an engaging and creative subject for young learners. The platform's unique human-and-AI teaching model helps develop critical problem-solving and logical thinking skills, while its community-driven hackathons and National Coding Olympiad motivate kids to showcase their real-world projects and build strong technological portfolios.
